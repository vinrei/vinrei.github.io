Account Sign-In and Data Backup

The Application offers an optional sign-in feature so that you can back up your data and synchronise it across your devices. Signing in is not required to use the Application, and you can use all of its core features without an account.

If you choose to sign in using a third-party provider (Sign in with Google or Sign in with Apple), that provider shares certain account information with the Application, which may include your email address, your name (where provided), and a unique account identifier. Sign-in and account identity are managed using Firebase Authentication. If you use Sign in with Apple, you may choose to hide your email address, in which case Apple provides a private relay email address instead. The Service Provider does not receive or store your password; authentication is handled entirely by your chosen sign-in provider.

When you are signed in, the information you create in the Application — such as your saved trigger foods and your app preferences (for example, measurement units and colour settings) — is stored on the Service Provider's cloud database, hosted on Amazon Web Services, so that it can be backed up and synchronised to your devices. This synchronised data is associated with your account's unique identifier.
