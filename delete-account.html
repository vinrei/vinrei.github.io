Deleting your Fodmate account and data

This page explains how to request deletion of your account and associated data in the Fodmate app, provided by Vincent Reilly.

How to delete your account

In the app (fastest):

  1. Open Fodmate and open the side menu.
  2. Tap Account & backup.
  3. Tap Delete account and confirm.

For your security, account deletion requires a recent sign-in. If prompted, sign out, sign back in, and then tap Delete account.

By email: If you can't access the app, email reilly.vincent+fodmate@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account and request deletion. We will action your request within a reasonable time.

What is deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes:

  • Your account / sign-in identity (the email and name associated with your Sign in with Google or Sign in with Apple account).
  • All data backed up to that account on our servers — your saved trigger foods, app preferences (such as measurement units and colour settings), and your foods-viewed history.

What is kept

  • On-device data: any data stored only on your phone or tablet remains on that device. To remove it, delete the app, or use the in-app controls (for example, removing individual trigger foods) before deleting your account.
  • Anonymised analytics and crash data: aggregated, anonymised usage and diagnostic data that is not linked to your account may be retained.

Retention period

Your account and backed-up data are deleted promptly when you use the in-app Delete account option, and within a reasonable time for email requests. Residual copies in encrypted, automated database backups are removed within 35 days.
